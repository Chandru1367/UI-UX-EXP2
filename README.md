## Aim

To design a professional and modern logo for CodeCraft, representing creativity, software development, innovation, and digital transformation while establishing a strong and memorable brand identity across various platforms.

## Algorithm
Apply Gestalt principles to create a visually appealing and meaningful logo.
Design a unique monogram using the initials CC to strengthen brand recognition.
Utilize modern typography and a balanced color palette to enhance visual appeal.
Ensure the logo remains scalable and adaptable for digital and print media.
Create the final logo in Canva with consistent branding guidelines and design standards.

## Output

## a. Gestalt Principles (Logo Design)

Proximity:
The "CodeCraft" text and CC monogram are placed closely together to establish a clear relationship between the symbol and brand name.

Similarity:
A consistent blue gradient color scheme is applied throughout the logo to create visual unity and reinforce the technology theme.

Figure-Ground:
The blue logo elements contrast effectively against a white or dark background, ensuring strong visibility and readability.

Closure:
The interconnected CC monogram forms a recognizable geometric shape, allowing viewers to perceive a complete and unified symbol.

Continuity:
Smooth curved lines within the monogram naturally guide the viewer's eye through the design, creating a sense of flow and balance.

## b. UI Elements & Design Patterns (Logo)
Geometric CC monogram design
Modern sans-serif typography
Blue gradient technology-inspired color palette
Minimal and professional visual style
Symbol-based branding suitable for software and tech products
Strong visual hierarchy between icon and brand name

## c. Interaction & Usability (Logo Usage)

Consistent appearance across digital and print materials
